HCC 19: Diabetes without Complication
ICD-10: E11.9, E10.9, E13.9
Description: Type 1 and Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus without complications. Most common HCC in Medicare Advantage.

HCC 18: Diabetes with Chronic Complication
ICD-10: E11.40, E11.41, E10.40
Description: Diabetes with chronic kidney disease or neuropathy. Higher RAF than HCC 19.

HCC 85: Congestive Heart Failure
ICD-10: I50.9, I50.1, I50.20
Description: Heart failure. High RAF weight, frequently under-coded.

HCC 96: Atrial Fibrillation
ICD-10: I48.0, I48.11, I48.19
Description: Atrial fibrillation and flutter. Requires annual recapture.

HCC 111: COPD
ICD-10: J44.0, J44.1, J44.9
Description: Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease. Frequently missed.

HCC 136: Chronic Kidney Disease Stage 5
ICD-10: N18.5, N18.6
Description: Advanced CKD. Very high RAF weight.

HCC 22: Morbid Obesity
ICD-10: E66.01, E66.09
Description: BMI 40 plus. Requires BMI documentation in chart.

HCC 59: Major Depressive Disorder
ICD-10: F32.0, F32.1, F32.2
Description: Depression. Frequently missed in primary care.

HCC 108: Vascular Disease
ICD-10: I70.201, I70.0, I73.9
Description: Peripheral vascular disease and atherosclerosis.

HCC 137: Chronic Kidney Disease Stage 4
ICD-10: N18.4
Description: Severe CKD one stage below ESRD.
